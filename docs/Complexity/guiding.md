<h2>Complexity | Guiding Questions</h2>



<div class="question-group">

  <div class="main-question">
    How can <u>complexity</u> be made <i>manageable</i> in empirical research?
  </div>

  <div class="sub-question">
   On which dimensions of complexity does the research <u>focus</u> and which dimensions remain in the background?
  </div>

  <div class="sub-question">
    Where, how, and to what extent does the <u>reduction of complexity</u> take place throughout the research process?
  </div>

</div>

<div class="question-group">

  <div class="main-question">
    To what extent does the project embrace <i>multiperspectivity</i>?
  </div>

  <div class="sub-question">
    Which perspectives inform the conceptualization, analysis, and interpretation of the research?
  </div>

 <div class="guiding-example-annotation">
